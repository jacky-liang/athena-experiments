Welcome to the ATHENA Experiment!

This is a 10 minute trial to gather data about how humans approach productivity. We hope to provide a tool that will allow people to upgrade their personal productivity by learning how each individual likes to work. The purpose of this experiment is to gather some preliminary data so that we can analyze the viability of this product.

Instructions:

You will be asked to complete two tasks that will switch automatically.

Task 1: Words

You will have to type the characters that appear on screen in order and you will recieve points based on the number you complete.

Task 2: Circles

You will have to drag different colored circles to the other ones of the matching color and size.
